ABOUT

Hi there! I'm Beau Jack, creator of all of the music you will find on this website.

I still remember watching Star Wars for the very first time when I was a child, and enjoying the galaxy that the music put me in. From the 'Title' piece to 'Duel of the Fates', John Williams has influenced my musical creations at every step of the way.

Follow this by growing up in an incredibly diverse music scene, ranging from Metal, Rock, Blues, Pop, R&B to Jazz and dedicating my life to music from secondary school onwards, I now have the tools and understanding to create the music that you need.

After achieving a distinction in an Extended Diploma in Music Performance & Production, I am now studying for a BMus (Hons) in Popular Music Performance degree in Brighton.

I love experimenting with music, stretching it to it limits of understanding yet still creating both an enjoyable experience for the listener and enhancing the visual piece that supports the music.

- Beau
